Method

Transparent assumptions, not a black box

Groundwork uses simplified emission factors to build intuition. It is meant for awareness and planning, not certified reporting.

Calculation assumptions
  • Travel: 0.192 kg/km petrol car, 0.171 diesel, 0.103 two-wheeler, 0.105 bus, 0.041 metro/train, around 500 kg per domestic round-trip flight.
  • Energy: 0.82 kg/kWh for India grid average or 0.475 kg/kWh for global average; around 42 kg CO2e per 14.2 kg LPG cylinder.
  • Diet: around 3.3 kg/day meat-heavy, 2.5 mixed, 1.7 vegetarian, 1.5 vegan, applied across 30 days.
  • Waste: around 0.5 kg CO2e per kg landfilled per week, reduced 40% if composted or recycled.
  • The 190 kg/month benchmark is a rough monthly slice of a 2.3 t CO2e/year per-person budget.

Nothing you enter is sent anywhere. The calculator runs in your browser, and the session log clears on refresh.
