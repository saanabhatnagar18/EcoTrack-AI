Method

Transparent assumptions, not a black box

Carbon Compass uses accessible emission factors to help you understand the difference between your daily habits and a simple per-person monthly benchmark.

Calculation details
  • Travel factors: petrol 0.192 kg/km, diesel 0.171 kg/km, two-wheeler 0.103 kg/km, bus 0.105 kg/km, train 0.041 kg/km, domestic flight 500 kg per round trip.
  • Energy factors: 0.475 kg CO₂e per kWh, 42 kg per LPG cylinder.
  • Diet factors: 3.3 kg/day meat-heavy, 2.5 mixed, 1.7 vegetarian, 1.5 vegan.
  • Waste factors: 0.5 kg CO₂e per kg landfilled, reduced by the recycled/composted share.
  • Target: 190 kg CO₂e per month is a rough personal benchmark for a low-carbon lifestyle.

All calculations happen in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.
